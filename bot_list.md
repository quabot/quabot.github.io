QuaBot is a next-generation Discord bot built to make server management easier, smoother, and to allow you to manage your server, your way. No need for 12 different bots, just use QuaBot to handle all the tasks. Use our easy-to-use and fast dashboard to quickly and easily configure our 80+ commands and features. We have the following modules:

### Modules

• **Tickets:** Create tickets with our 100% customizable tickets system filled with automation, customization and easy-of-use.
• **Statistics** Channels: Display server statistics at the top of your channel list.
• **Suggestions:** Let users suggest new features and ideas.
• **Givewaways:** Create custom giveaways in your server.
• **Reaction Roles:** Use dropdowns & buttons to grant users the roles they want and control who can get what roles.
• **Welcomer:** Welcome your new members with custom messages & images.
• **Security & Moderation:** Our advanced moderation suite allows you to moderate your server with moderation rules to automatically punish users, and easily manage moderation cases between staff members.
• **Auto Purge & Auto Delete:** Clear & Manage channels to keep them nice and clean.
• **Verification:** Users must complete captchas before getting access to the server. Customize what type of verification to use.
• **Action Logs:** View deleted messages, edited messages and other actions in your server.
• **Starboard:** Showcase important messages that users can vote on.
• **Boost Messages:** Send a message when someone boosts your server.
• **Advanced Message Sender:** Craft custom messages that you can edit later on.

…and more on the way. QuaBot keeps expanding with new tools and smarter automations so you can focus on your community instead of micromanaging 12 different bots.
